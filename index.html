WHAT WE DO

RxPRESS is revolutionizing the pharmacy experience by streamlining customer interactions through QR code technology, which allows users to check in digitally and bypass long lines. Upon scanning a QR code, pharmacy staff receive instant notifications to prepare medications, significantly reducing pick-up wait times and enhancing overall efficiency. Customers enjoy real-time updates on their order status, while the app's user-friendly interface and features like refill reminders cater to users of all ages. With a strong emphasis on health data security, RxPRESS ensures that personal information is handled securely. The impact on pharmacy operations is profound, as it reduces foot traffic, fosters customer satisfaction, and provides valuable insights into customer behavior, ultimately setting a new standard for pharmacy services and improving both customer experience and operational effectiveness.

SERVICES

  • QR Code Check-In

    Skip the line with our convenient QR code check-in system. Scan the code upon arrival to notify pharmacy staff, ensuring a swift and seamless experience.

  • Copy of Purchase Transaction History

    Patients' transaction history will be at hand as clients will be able to conveniently monitor the specific type and quantity of medicine used in purchase history.

  • Availability of Medicine

    When the need is physically low, low-volume medicine may be available, and the nearest locations of pharmacies and branches will be shown and recommended by our app.

  • Notifications

    Doctors and patients alike will receive different types of notifications. Doctors are notified of their meds and which medicines their patients require, while patients receive reminders to buy their meds.
